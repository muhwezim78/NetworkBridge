# Privacy Policy for TerraConnect Uganda

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-17

TerraConnect Uganda ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide securely, including:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, and phone number when you register.
- **Payment Information:** Phone numbers and transaction records for mobile money or card payments (processed securely via ZengaPay).

### B. Network & Device Information

To provide improved network management and automation, the App collects:

- **Router Credentials:** IP addresses, usernames, and passwords for the MikroTik routers you manage via the App. These credentials are transmitted securely to our backend infrastructure to enable remote management features. They are encrypted at rest.
- **Network Traffic & Usage Data:** Bandwidth usage, active user lists (Active Hotspot/PPPoE Users), and voucher/profile data.
- **Device Identifiers:** Basic information about the mobile device (model, OS version) for analytics and troubleshooting.

### C. Permissions

The App requires the following Android permissions strictly for functionality:

- **INTERNET:** To communicate with our secure backend servers and your managed routers.
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE & ACCESS_WIFI_STATE:** To check internet connectivity and verify local network status.

**Note:** We do **not** access your precise location (GPS) or contacts.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

- **Provide Services:** Allow you to configure ISPs, manage MikroTik hotspots, create vouchers, and monitor PPPoE users remotely.
- **Process Payments:** Facilitate secure subscription and voucher purchases via integrated payment gateways.
- **Improve Performance:** Analyze app stability, identify bugs via crash reporting, and optimize network responsiveness.
- **Communication:** Send critical service alerts, billing notifications, and support messages.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Encryption:** All sensitive data, including router passwords and API keys, is encrypted during transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and storage.
- **Access Control:** We implement strict access controls and industry-standard security protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We may share limited data with trusted third-party providers solely for operational purposes:

- **ZengaPay:** For processing secure mobile money and card transactions.
- **Google Play Services:** For app distribution, updates, and anonymized analytics.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

## 5. Your Rights & Control

You have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements).

To exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date".
